🌍 EcoTwin – Your AI-Powered Sustainability Companion

EcoTwin is an AI-powered carbon footprint tracking platform that helps individuals understand, monitor, and reduce their environmental impact through personalized insights, voice-enabled AI interactions, and a living virtual companion called the Carbon Twin.

Unlike traditional carbon calculators, EcoTwin transforms sustainability awareness into an engaging daily experience by combining AI, gamification, and behavioral insights.

🚀 Problem Statement

Many individuals are unaware of how their daily habits contribute to carbon emissions. Even when they are aware, existing solutions often fail to provide actionable guidance and long-term engagement.

EcoTwin bridges this gap by making sustainability:

Personalized
Interactive
Data-driven
Rewarding
✨ Key Features
🌱 Carbon Twin

A dynamic virtual companion whose emotions, appearance, and environment evolve based on the user's carbon footprint.

Happy and thriving when emissions are reduced
Concerned when emissions increase
Real-time visual feedback for sustainable actions
Daily evolution based on user habits
🎙️ Gemini Live Sustainability Coach

A voice-enabled AI assistant that allows users to:

Log daily activities through conversation
Receive personalized eco-friendly recommendations
Learn about sustainability concepts
Track environmental impact naturally

Example:

"I traveled by bus and ate vegetarian meals today."

The assistant automatically updates the user's carbon profile.
